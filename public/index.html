Your Safety is Our Priority

At Pocket Companion, we take your safety seriously. Our CONNECT feature is designed with safety as the foundation of every interaction.

Safety Guidelines for CONNECT

  • Group Meetings Only: All meetups through CONNECT are organized as group activities. Never arrange private, one-on-one meetings with someone you've met through the app.
  • Public Places: All group meetups take place in public locations with multiple people present, ensuring a safe and comfortable environment.
  • Inform Trusted Contacts: Before attending any group meetup, inform a friend or family member about your plans, including the location, time, and expected duration.
  • Protect Your Information: Be cautious about sharing personal details such as your full address, workplace, or financial information until you've built trust within the group.
  • Report Concerns: If you encounter any suspicious behavior or feel uncomfortable, report it immediately through the app's safety features.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something doesn't feel right, remove yourself from the situation and contact support.
  • Start Small: Begin with larger group activities and gradually build relationships within the community before joining smaller groups.

We've designed CONNECT specifically to encourage group interactions because we believe that genuine connections happen best in safe, supportive group environments. By focusing on shared interests and group activities, we create opportunities for meaningful friendships to develop naturally while maintaining your safety and privacy.
